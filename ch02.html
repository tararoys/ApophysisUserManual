Introduction to Grand Julias

Written by Tara Roys, Copyright 2006-2009

Julia fractals are some of the most interesting fractals to create in Apophysis. They are also very frustrating if you don't know where to begin. Today I will teach you a good technique for creating something called a Grand Julia--one of those Julia fractals that looks awesomely complex but is fairly simple to create.

This tutorial is an easy tutorial and assumes you know nothing about Apophysis. As a result, it explains everything you need to do in great detail. There are some parts that you won't care about or don't needed if you are a more experienced user. The tutorial will tell you when those parts are coming if you care to skip them.

This tutorial was written with Apophysis Version 2.08 beta 2 in mind, although it works for the Apophysis 3D hack as well.
