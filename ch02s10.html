Moving Transforms to Their Final Positions

Now is when the magic happens: i.e. we get to turn the previous section's rather boring-looking circle into the Grand Julia shown above.

The only thing we have left to do is to move the transforms into their final locations.

Click on the first triangle and shrink it by 300% . Do this by using the shrinking dialog in the transform tab.. The smaller triangle button on the left-hand side is the shrink button, while the larger triangle button is the enlarge button. In order to shrink it, use the smaller triangle.

Click on the second transform and move it up one unit. Use the translate dialog in the triangle tab .

Click on the third transform , rotate it 90 degrees counterclockwise, and shrink it by 150%

Click on the fourth transform and rotate it by 135 degrees clockwise and scale it down by 400% .

Above are the final movements if you simply want to type them in.
