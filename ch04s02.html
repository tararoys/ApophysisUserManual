Picking a Gradient

Ok, now we get to the fun part. Coloring your triangles! Click on the gradient button.

Choose a gradient you like. For quilts, the more detailed the gradient is- i.e. the more bands of color it has- the more detailed your quilt will be. I'm going to use preset 346_4u0012.jpg because I like blues and purples.
