Introduction

One of the most important parts of your fractal is its color scheme. In Apophysis, we change the color scheme by changing the gradient. Apophysis comes with several preloaded gradients and a bunch of tools for editing them, but most people feel that Apophysis's editing tools don't do exactly what they want. What if you want a very specific color scheme that isn't listed in the gradient editor? In that case, you will need to create your own gradient. This chapter discusses a few methods of how to do this. The first is using a program called ApoMap. The second is editing parameter sets directly by hand. Let's get to it!
